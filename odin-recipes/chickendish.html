Grandma Opsahl's Chicken Dish

A picture of Grandma Opsahls famous chicken dish!

This is a family favorite. It is extremely easy to make with minimal ingredients. It tastes amazing and is a comfort food that we keep in our meal rotation.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chicken thighs
  • 1 Can cream of chicken soup
  • 1 box of stove top stuffing
  • 1 Package of swiss cheese slices

Steps

  • Preheat oven to 350Â°
  • Place chicken thighs in oven safe cooking dish
  • Place a layer of swiss cheese slices over the thighs
  • Spread the cream of chicken soup on top of the cheese
  • Spread the stuffing mix on top of the soup
  • Cover with aluminum foil
  • Place in oven for at least an hour or until chicken is cooked through
  • Take aluminum foil off and cook an additional 5-10 minutes
  • Remove and devour
