Grandma Opsahl's Chicken Dish

A picture of Grandma Opsahls famous chicken dish!

This is a family favorite. It is extremely easy to make with minimal ingredients. It tastes amazing and is a comfort food that we keep in our meal rotation.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chicken thighs
  • 1 Can cream of chicken soup
  • 1 box of stove top stuffing
  • 1 Package of swiss cheese slices

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°
  2. Place chicken thighs in oven safe cooking dish
  3. Place a layer of swiss cheese slices over the thighs
  4. Spread the cream of chicken soup on top of the cheese
  5. Spread the stuffing mix on top of the soup
  6. Cover with aluminum foil
  7. Place in oven for at least an hour or until chicken is cooked through
  8. Take aluminum foil off and cook an additional 5-10 minutes
  9. Remove and devour
